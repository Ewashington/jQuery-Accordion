Spaghetti Recipe

Ingredients

  1. 1 pound ground beef 90% Lean Ground Beef Sirloin 2.25 lbs
  2. 1 onion, chopped
  3. 4 cloves garlic, minced
  4. 1 small green bell pepper, diced
  5. 1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  6. 1 (16 ounce) can tomato sauce
  7. 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  8. 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  9. 2 teaspoons dried basil
  10. 1 teaspoon salt
  11. 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  12. Great Value Pure Ground Black Pepper, 3 oz

Directions

Combine ground beef, onion, garlic, and green pepper in a large saucepan. Cook and stir until meat is brown and vegetables are tender. Drain grease. Stir diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste into the pan. Season with oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer spaghetti sauce for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
